Privacy Policy

Overview

Xyloh.ai provides conversational assistance and experimental features (“Services”). This page explains what we collect, why we collect it, and your choices.

What We Collect

  • Session Data: A user-provided session ID (e.g., email/handle) used to save conversation history to our key-value store.
  • Messages: Text you send and AI replies, stored to deliver continuity and let you revisit past conversations.
  • Basic Analytics: Minimal usage metrics (e.g., request counts, timestamps) for reliability and abuse prevention.
  • Optional Sources: If you use search-powered features, queries are sent to trusted third-party APIs (e.g., news/medical) to fetch citations.

How We Use Data

  • Provide and improve the Services (e.g., load your history, generate responses, fetch citations).
  • Maintain security and prevent abuse.
  • Fix bugs, monitor uptime, and understand aggregate usage (non-identifying).

What We Don’t Do

  • No sale of personal data.
  • No ad-tech trackers.
  • No scraping of your external accounts.

Third-Party Services

When enabled by you, we may call external APIs (e.g., OpenAI for model inference, news/medical/legal sources for citations). Those services process your queries under their own terms and privacy policies.

Your Choices

  • Use a pseudonymous session ID (not an email) if you prefer.
  • Request deletion of stored conversation history by contacting us at hello@xyloh.ai.
  • Limit use of search-powered features if you don’t want queries sent to third-party APIs.

Data Retention

Conversation history is retained to provide continuity, but we cap storage and periodically purge old items. You can request deletion at any time.

Security

We use modern cloud infrastructure and limit access to environment secrets and data. No system is perfectly secure; report issues to security@xyloh.ai.

Children

The Services are not directed to children under 13. If you believe we’ve collected data from a child, contact us for removal.

Updates

We may update this policy as the product evolves. We’ll revise the “Last updated” date and, for material changes, may provide additional notice.
